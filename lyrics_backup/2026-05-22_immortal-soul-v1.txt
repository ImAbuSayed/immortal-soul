[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Akhiyan de vich teri tasveer main rakhda haan, falling so deep.
Tere nakhre main has ke jarda haan sohniye, losing my sleep.
Saari raat main tera takda haan raah billo, next to me.
Tere utte mar mitda haan main sachhi, wild energy.

[Hook 2]
Nede nede aaja sohniye tu mere kol, let me hold you close.
Dil da haal sunaunda haan main ajj tainu, giving you the most.
Zulfaan di chaa vich baithda haan main kalla, fading away.
Tera nasha chadhda janda ae mainu nitt, day by day.

[Verse 1]
Hanera kalla lagda ae mainu hun raat nu, shadows on the wall.
Main dil nu samjhaunda haan roz tere layi, waiting for a call.
Hawaavan vich ghuldi teri mehek sohniye, sweet perfume.
Kalla beh ke takda haan main teri raah, across the room.
Kach de pyale vich dulldi aa yaad teri, pouring my pain.
Bahar challe thandi hawa seene vich agg, standing in the rain.
Tainu yaad karda haan main sachi ajj vi, missing your touch.
Par tu kade mudke na aayi mere kol, hurts me so much.

[Pre-Chorus]
Akhiyan ch hanju mere sukde nahi kade, running dry.
Saah mere tere bina chalde nahi hun, tell me why.
Raatan di neend meri ud gayi ae kite, far away.
Tere bina jeenda haan main bas aivein, fading gray.

[Chorus]
Dil mera mangda ae tera pyar sohniye, gimme your love.
Udd-da main firda haan tere khayalan ch, flying above.
Naina de teer tu maare mere seene te, shooting me down.
Tere piche ghumda haan main pagal jeha, all over town.
Rooh meri kardi ae fariyad rab aage, hear my voice.
Tere bina mera naiyo sarda kade vi, got no choice.
Hathan vich hath paake chal mere naal, hold me tight.
Ban ja tu meri heeriye is raat layi, into the night.

[Verse 2]
Garam lohe di dhadkan sun saddi gaddi di, roaring so loud.
Kaale sheeshe thalle lukda haan main sab ton, escaping the crowd.
Dhooye de badal vich ud-da haan main ucha, off the ground.
Dil vich agg lagi ae mere sachi, hear the sound.
Zehar wargi teri aashiqui ae mere layi, toxic game.
Yaaran layi khad-da haan main sacche dil naal, no fake fame.
Baraf wargi teri tasveer mere dil vich, cold to the bone.
Main kalla hi lad-da haan is jagg naal, fighting alone.

[Verse 3]
Hun main badal gaya haan is waqt naal, turning the page.
Dil wale pinjre cho ud gya main azaad, leaving the cage.
Garam engine di tapash sahe mera jism, feeling the heat.
Mere pairaan thalle baldi zameen agg wargi, watch my feet.
Teri khedan da main shikaar nahi hun kade, broken free.
Hathan diyan leekan ch tu nahi mere naal, meant to be.
Mere piche laggiyan nazraan kayi duniya di, shining bright.
Hanju naiyo milde hun meriyan akhan ch, I own the night.

[Bridge]

Every single breath we take is building the desire
Come a little closer, let Saeed take control
You and I together, connecting to the soul
Whisper all your secrets, leave the world behind
I can read the rhythm of your beautiful mind

[Final Chorus]
