Hanera kalla lagda ae mainu hun raat nu, shadows on the wall.
Main dil nu samjhaunda haan roz tere layi, waiting for a call.
Hawaavan vich ghuldi teri mehek sohniye, sweet perfume.
Kalla beh ke takda haan main teri raah, across the room.
Kach de pyale vich dulldi aa yaad teri, pouring my pain.
Bahar challe thandi hawa seene vich agg, standing in the rain.
Tainu yaad karda haan main sachi ajj vi, missing your touch.
Par tu kade mudke na aayi mere kol, hurts me so much.
